Dr. N L Muralikrishna B.E., M. Tech, Ph.D


Controller of Examinations
Professor in Industrial and Production Engineering Department
PESCE, Mandya.

Dr. N L Muralikrishna was earlier Head of the department of Industrial & Production Engineering and now officiating as COE. He has obtained Ph D degree in Mechanical Engineering (Micro Finishing) from VTU University in the Year 2016, M.Tech in Tribology from IIT Delhi in the year 2000. As a research student along with his guide, he has developed an Extrusion honing machine for his Ph.D. Research work. He is having a teaching experience of 27 years and has taught various subjects to the Industrial and Production engineering Students.
